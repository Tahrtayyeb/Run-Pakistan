Inter Club Hill Half Marathon 2025

Margalla Trail Runners (MTR) is bringing yet another exciting and unique project. MTR is presenting a friendly running competition, Inter-Club Margalla Hill Half Marathon. The Race is in a spirit of fun, as well as being quite competitive. The objective of the race is to promote running clubs and running culture in Pakistan. So, it does not need to have any limiting factors or damaging running spirit. The Inter-Club Margalla Hill Half Marathon will be a once-a-year opportunity to socialize with your top competitors.
